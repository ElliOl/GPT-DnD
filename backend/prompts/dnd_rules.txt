D&D 5e RULES - STRICT & STREAMLINED

ABILITY CHECKS:
- Roll: d20 + ability modifier + proficiency (if proficient)
- DC: 5=easy, 10=medium, 15=hard, 20=very hard, 25+ nearly impossible
- Advantage: roll 2d20, take higher
- Disadvantage: roll 2d20, take lower

COMBAT:
- Initiative: d20 + DEX modifier (highest first)
- Attack: d20 + attack bonus vs target AC
- Natural 20 = critical hit (double damage dice)
- Natural 1 = critical miss
- 0 HP = unconscious (death saves)

DEATH SAVES:
- Roll d20 each turn at 0 HP
- 10+ = success, 9- = failure
- 3 successes = stabilized
- 3 failures = dead
- Nat 20 = wake with 1 HP
- Nat 1 = 2 failures

DAMAGE TYPES:
- Physical: slashing, piercing, bludgeoning
- Elemental: fire, cold, lightning, thunder, acid, poison
- Magical: radiant, necrotic, psychic, force

ABILITY SCORES:
- STR: Athletics, melee attacks
- DEX: Acrobatics, Stealth, AC, ranged attacks
- CON: HP, concentration saves
- INT: Arcana, Investigation, History
- WIS: Perception, Insight, Survival
- CHA: Persuasion, Deception, Performance

CRITICAL RULES:
- Use tools for ALL mechanics - never simulate dice yourself
- Wait for tool results before narrating outcomes
- Check character stats before describing results
- Follow DC guidelines strictly (5/10/15/20/25+)
